About Us

The Puppy Path is a nonprofit dog adoption organization based in Calamba, Laguna . Our mission is to rescue dogs from the streets and provide loving homes to pets surrendered by previous owners. We are committed to give every dog a second chance to find a family that will offer them the care and affection they deserve.

Whether you're looking to adopt or support our cause, join us in creating a better world for these lovable dogs in need!

Need to Re-home your pet?

We're here to help! Our doors are open to provide them with a safe and loving environment.
